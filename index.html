Data & Sources

Crime & dispatch data

  • Central Utah 911 dispatch records, Jan 2020 – May 2026
  • Obtained under Utah GRAMA (Government Records Access & Management Act), retention series 27585 — certified true copy, June 1, 2026
  • 692,841 dispatch calls processed; filtered to criminal-nature incidents
  • This map plots 51,012 criminal incidents across 7,659 distinct locations

How "crime" is defined

  • Includes: theft, burglary, assault, fraud, drug offenses, vandalism, threats, domestic incidents, and similar criminal-nature calls
  • Excludes: traffic stops, medical & fire assists, alarms, welfare checks, and informational calls
  • Reports filed at police stations or institutional facilities are flagged separately so facility addresses do not appear as crime hotspots

Geocoding

  • Street addresses converted to coordinates with the U.S. Census Bureau geocoding service (76% street-level match; unmatched addresses omitted)

Apartment communities

  • 32 communities compiled and verified via Apartments.com and Google Maps, June 2026
  • Class A–D reflects market tier (A = newest / highest-end), assigned from age, amenities, and resident ratings
Class ratings are a preliminary assessment pending review. Dispatch calls reflect reported activity, not convictions or verified offenses.
